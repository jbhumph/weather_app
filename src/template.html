Weather Compare

Weather Compare is an application providing access to current weather data, allowing you to compare weather conditions across multiple locations. To your right, choose between direct comparison mode or detail view. Then begin by entering a city name.
Compare View
This view allows you to compare the primary details of the two sets of weather data side-by-side.
Detail View
The detail view allows you to get a more detailed view of each location, one at a time. Switch with the arrow button.
